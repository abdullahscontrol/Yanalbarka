    About Collage

    As its unique name reveals, `Yan Albarka College is an inimitable school set to produce a well knowledgeable, responsible, principled virtuos generation.
    As an idea with paetial application, the philosophy of the school initiated by Dr. MN Abdullahi over decades before it comes to full implementation under `yan albarka college in 2020.

    Aims and Objectives


    The general aim of the forum is to guide and produce a responsible and virtuous young generation among the Muslim Umma
    that will aspire for the best life in this world and the hereafter.

    Specisically, the forum Objectives are as follows:

    1) Teach children the basics and essentials of Islamic knowledge, manners and skills that are to be mastered right from childhood
    2) Strengthen children`s love and relationship with their Creator, their Prophet (Peace and blessing of Allah be upon him), his household, and companions and inspire them to uphold the Qur’an and the Sunnah as the Original and primary sources of knowledge and guidance.
    3) Develop appreciation for ICT in all aspect of life and enhance the capacity of children to leverage on new media for maximum achievement.
    4) Train members to become leading figures and role models in their schools and society at large.
    5) Monitor the effects of peer groups on members of the forum and provide guidance in the selection of peers.
    6) Teach children rational thinking and the technical skills needed to confront the vicissitudes of life.
    7) Reinstate the primacy of sound and deep – rooted knowledge in the minds of youths and inspire them to be earnest in pursuing post – secondary education.
    8) Maximize the potentials of gifted children and cater for the needs of children with learning difficulties.
    9) Prepare teenage members for university and other institutions.
    10) Provide guidance and counseling to parents in order to enhance effective child upbringing.
